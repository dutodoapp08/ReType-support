FAQ

Q. Does ReType diagnose my MBTI in the app?
A. You can either declare your type (if you already know it) or go through a short onboarding quiz to set a baseline. ReType is not a medical or clinical diagnostic tool.
Q. How do I cancel my subscription?
A. On your iPhone: Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions. Manage or cancel your ReType subscription there (per App Store rules).
Q. Where is my data stored?
A. In the current version, data is stored locally on your device. We do not sync your answers or type data to the cloud unless we announce otherwise.
Q. I found a bug. What should I send?
A. Screenshots, when it happened, steps to reproduce, and your device and iOS version are very helpful.
